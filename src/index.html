FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before you lace up. Can't find your answer? Reach out and we'll get back to you.

Contact Us

  • Head to our Register page, pick your division, and complete the sign-up form. You can register as a full team or as a free agent and we'll place you on a roster that fits your skill level.

  • Yes. Free agents are welcome every season. We'll match you with a team based on your skill level and schedule preferences. Most free agents end up returning with the same squad the following season.

  • New teams fill out a skill assessment during registration. Returning teams are placed based on their previous season record and performance. We evaluate placements after week two and will adjust teams if needed to keep games competitive.

  • Every game has an official scorekeeper tracking points, rebounds, assists, steals, and blocks for every player. Stats are uploaded to the league portal within 48 hours of each game and are visible to everyone.

  • Full season schedules are released one week before the season starts. You'll receive an email with your game dates, times, and gym locations. Schedules are also available in the league portal at any time.

  • Teams must have a minimum of five players to start a game. If you're short, you can pull from our free agent pool with 24 hours notice. Forfeits are recorded as a loss and repeated forfeits may affect your playoff seeding.

  • All registered teams qualify for the playoffs. Seeding is based on regular season record. The playoff bracket is single elimination across two weeks, with the championship game and ring ceremony at the end of the final week.

  • Full refunds are available up to 14 days before the season starts. After that, we offer a 50% credit toward the next season. No refunds are issued once the season is underway. Contact us at support@recessleagues.com with any questions.
